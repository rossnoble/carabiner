Headstand

Welcome to Headstand

Headstand is a collection of lightweight boilerplate [Sass](http://sass-lang.com) files for new projects. It's not a full fledged framework; it's a starting point.

All of the files includes in the library are designed to be modified and altered as you craft the look and feel of user interface.
